Overview

Purpose

This website aims to provide a step-by-step guide for individuals facing a similar situation as mine, helping them understand the process of creating my personal Monopoly game, which I've named Massive Monopoly. It is designed to offer valuable insights into what to do and what to avoid during the game's development. Please note that this website will not share any completed components of my Monopoly game without a watermark to discourage unauthorized copying and usage.

Audience

Anyone 13+ who wishes to create their own Monopoly game.
